---
title: Histórico do Envelope
description: GET /api/envelopes/{envelopeId}/history retorna os logs de acesso/ação.
openapi: 'GET /api/envelopes/{envelopeId}/history'
---

Endpoint: `GET /api/envelopes/{envelopeId}/history`

Auth: `Authorization: Bearer <token>`

Erros comuns:
- 400 — envelopeId ausente
- 500 — erro interno ao buscar histórico
