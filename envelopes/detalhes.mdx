---
title: Obter Envelope
description: GET /api/envelopes/{envelopeId} retorna os dados completos do envelope.
openapi: 'GET /api/envelopes/{envelopeId}'
---

Endpoint: `GET /api/envelopes/{envelopeId}`

Auth: `Authorization: Bearer <token>`

Erros comuns:
- 400 — envelopeId ausente
- 404 — não encontrado
- 500 — erro interno
