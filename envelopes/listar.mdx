---
title: Listar Envelopes
description: GET /api/envelopes com paginação e filtros.
---

Endpoint: `GET /api/envelopes`

Query params:
- `pageSize` (int, padrão 10, máx 100)
- `page` (int, padrão 1)
- `searchText` (string, opcional)
- `lastUpdateStartDate` (string, opcional)
- `lastUpdateEndDate` (string, opcional)
- `status` (string, opcional)

Auth: `Authorization: Bearer <token>`
