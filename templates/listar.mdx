---
title: Listar Templates
description: GET /api/templates com paginação e filtros.
openapi: 'GET /api/templates'
---

Endpoint: `GET /api/templates`

Query params:
- `pageSize` (int, padrão 10, máx 100)
- `page` (int, padrão 1)
- `searchText` (string, opcional)
- `lastUpdateStartDate`, `lastUpdateEndDate` (string, opcional)

Auth: `Authorization: Bearer <token>`
