---
title: Envelopes
description: Endpoints para envio, reenvio, cancelamento e consulta de envelopes.
---

Autenticação: `Authorization: Bearer <token>`

## GET /api/envelopes — Listar
Query: `page`, `pageSize`, `searchText`, `lastUpdateStartDate`, `lastUpdateEndDate`, `status`.

## GET /api/envelopes/{envelopeId} — Obter

## GET /api/envelopes/{envelopeId}/history — Histórico

## POST /api/envelopes/send — Enviar
Body mínimo: `submissionType`, `documentSettings` + `s3Key`, `fields`, `recipients`.

## POST /api/envelopes/resend — Reenviar
Body: `{ "envelopeId": "..." }`

## POST /api/envelopes/void — Anular
Body: `{ "envelopeId": "...", "justificativa": "..." }`
